## Sales Call Structure

From KG/LP conversation - the demo call approach:

### 1. Discovery (10 min)
- What's your current ops setup?
- How many recurring contributors/vendors?
- What's painful right now? (invoicing, payouts, month-end, reporting)
- What tools are you using today?

### 2. Walk Through Services (10 min)
Go through each domain, describe the problem it solves:
- **Entity:** "You own a Swiss Association - privacy by default, you control it"
- **Workflows:** "Repeatable processes for invoicing, approvals, payouts, reporting"
- **Operator support:** "We run day-to-day so you don't need to"

### 3. Case Study (5 min)
Use Jetstream (for internal calls only):
> "Here's a team - 6 people, half located here, half located there. Didn't know what to do. We organized them, got it running for [X]/month. They're shipping product, not doing admin."

### 4. Fit Assessment (5 min)
- What would you need beyond what I described?
- What's your timeline for solving this?
- Who else would need to be involved in a decision?

### 5. Next Steps
- If fit: Proposal + onboarding timeline
- If not ready: Add to nurture, share content
